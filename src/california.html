McWay Falls

Description

McWay Falls is an extremeley short hike, but we think that it's an essential stop for every traveller who is passing through on Pacific Highway 1. It provides magnificent views both south and north of the viewpoint, though we recommend that you research to see if it is closed before making plans.

Location

The McWay Waterfall Trail is pretty far out there on Pacific Highway 1, almost 160 miles south of San Fransisco and nearly 300 miles north of Los Angeles. Located inside Julia Pfeiffer Burns State Park, it is well marked and easy to spot off the highway 37 miles south of Carmel.

Difficulty

EASY: This hike is for everyone! Under a mile length round trip, the views and trail are very accessible to those who don't have outstanding health problems.
